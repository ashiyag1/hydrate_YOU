Hydration hacks

Drink smarter, not harder.

Simple habits that make hitting your goal effortless every single day.

🌅
Morning glass ritual
Down a full glass before coffee each morning. It kickstarts metabolism and rehydrates after 8 hours of sleep.
🍽️
Pre-meal hydration
Drink a glass before every meal. It aids digestion, reduces overeating, and adds 600 ml to your daily total effortlessly.
⏰
90-minute reminders
Set a recurring alarm every 90 minutes. Small, frequent sips are more effective than guzzling large amounts at once.
🌡️
Weather-adjusted goal
Hot day or workout? Add 500–1000 ml to your goal. Your body loses significantly more water through sweat.
👁️
Keep it visible
Place a water bottle on your desk, bedside table, and in your bag. Out of sight really is out of mind with hydration.
🍉
Eat your water too
Watermelon, cucumber, oranges, and strawberries are 85–95% water. They count! So do herbal teas and coconut water.
